Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a TitleCheck report take?

expand_more
Most standard reports are delivered within 15 minutes. Complex historical properties may take up to 24 hours for our legal team to manually verify discrepancies.

Is this report legally binding for title insurance?

expand_more
TitleCheck provides informational reports used for due diligence. While highly accurate, we recommend providing our findings to your title insurance provider for final policy issuance.

What happens if a lien is discovered?

expand_more
Our report will detail the lien holder, the amount, and the filing date. We also provide a "Resolution Pathway" guide to help you or your attorney clear the title.
